## 💭 Why Compare These Two Films? 

At first glance, the films feel very different. One is psychological horror. The other is social drama. But beneath the surface, they wrestle with similar questions:
How does racism operate in everyday life?
What does it mean to exist between two worlds?
How do systems of power shape identity?

Both films highlight the tension between visibility and control, showing how Black identity is interpreted, restricted, or even commodified in different ways.
